The Foundation

What is Dharma?

Dharma is not merely a religion — it is the eternal order underlying all of existence. Rooted in the Sanskrit root dhṛ (to hold, to sustain), it represents the cosmic law that keeps the universe in balance.

"Dharma is that which supports, upholds, and nourishes the cosmos, society, and the individual simultaneously."

⚖️
Ṛta
Cosmic order and natural law governing all existence
🔥
Satya
Truth as the ultimate foundation of righteous action
🌸
Ahiṃsā
Non-violence as the highest moral and spiritual principle
♾️
Moksha
Liberation from the cycle of birth and rebirth
