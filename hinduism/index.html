Cosmic Chronology

The Four Yugas

Navigate the great wheel of time — from the golden age of Satya to the iron age of Kali — across billions of years of dharmic cosmology.

सत्य
Satya Yuga
1,728,000 Years
The age of truth and purity. Dharma stands on all four legs. Human lifespan spans 100,000 years. The age of Brahma, Vishnu, and the primordial sages.
त्रेता
Treta Yuga
1,296,000 Years
Three-quarters dharma. The age of great kings and epic deeds. Suryavanshi dynasties flourish. Maryada Purushottam Rama walks the earth.
द्वापर
Dwapara Yuga
864,000 Years
Half dharma remains. The age of the Mahabharata. Chandravanshi dynasties peak. Sri Krishna imparts the Bhagavad Gita on Kurukshetra.
कलि
Kali Yuga
432,000 Years
One quarter dharma. The age of strife and forgetting. Yet also the age in which the simplest devotion yields the greatest merit — and remembrance becomes revolution.

Creation by Brahma

The 65th king of the Suryavansha, Rama of Ayodhya, upholds cosmic dharma. Lanka falls. The ideal state — Rama Rajya — is established.

The Pandavas and Kauravas — both Chandravanshi — meet at Kurukshetra. The Gita is revealed. The age of warriors ends and dharma is recalibrated.

The Great Lineages

Trace the unbroken threads of divine ancestry — from the sun and moon to the sages and kings who shaped the subcontinent across ages.

☀️
Suryavansha
The Solar Dynasty — Born of the Sun
Descended from Surya (Sun God) through Manu Vaivasvata and Ikshvaku. The dynasty of Sri Rama, Harishchandra, and Dilipa. The most ancient royal line on earth, spanning from Treta Yuga to Kali Yuga.
120+ Monarchs Documented
 🌙
Chandravansha
The Lunar Dynasty — Born of the Moon
Descended from Chandra (Moon God) through Ila, Pururava, and Yayati. The dynasty of Sri Krishna, Arjuna, and the Pandavas. Split into Yadava, Kuru, Puru, and Anu branches — reshaping the world.
200+ Monarchs Documented
 ✨
Saptarishi Gotra
The Sage Lineages — Born of Brahma
The seven mind-born sons of Brahma whose cosmic lineages are the root of every brahmin gotra and royal family. From Kashyapa's progeny — all living beings descend. The cosmic preceptors across every Manvantara.
7 Primary + 49 Sub-Gotras
 🐍
Nagvansha
The Serpent Dynasty — Born of Kashyapa
Descended from sage Kashyapa and Kadru. The Naga kings — Takshaka, Vasuki, Shesha, and Ananta — ruled ancient kingdoms from Takshashila to Nagaland. Their lineage interweaves with every major dynasty of Bharat.
40+ Naga Dynasties Traced
 🔥
Agnivansha
The Fire Dynasty — Born of Sacred Flame
Descended from Agni (Fire God) through the sacrificial yagnas of ancient seers. The Agnivanshi clans — Paramaras, Chandellas, Pratiharas, and Chahamanas — rose as the great Rajput defenders of Dharma in the medieval age of Bharat.
60+ Agnivanshi Clans Traced
 🪷
Rishivanshi
The Sage-Born — Lineage of the Rishis
Communities claiming direct descent from the Saptarishis, Brahmarishis, and Maharishis of the Vedic age. Their lineages trace through sacred gotras — Bharadvaja, Atreya, Kashyapa, Gautama — linking every family back to the divine origins of creation.

Did You Know?

Fascinating Dharma Facts

The most ancient living civilization on earth holds secrets that continue to astonish the world.

Cosmology · Vedic Science
🌌 The Oldest Cosmology on Earth
The Vedic concept of the universe — 4.32 billion years for one Kalpa (day of Brahma) — is remarkably close to modern science's estimate of Earth's age at 4.5 billion years. Vedic rishis calculated this thousands of years before telescopes.
Mathematics · Sulba Sutras
🔢 Pythagoras Was Already Known in India
The Baudhayana Sulba Sutra (800 BCE) stated what the world calls the Pythagorean theorem — over 200 years before Pythagoras was born. Ancient Bharat also gave humanity the concept of zero, the decimal system, and infinity.
Medicine · Ayurveda
⚕️ Surgery Was Practiced 2,600 Years Ago
Sushruta — the Father of Surgery — performed rhinoplasty, cataract surgery, and C-sections over 2,600 years ago. His Sushruta Samhita describes 300+ surgical procedures and 120+ surgical instruments. Ancient India pioneered modern medicine.
Language · Sanskrit
🗣️ Sanskrit — The Language of Computers
NASA scientists have identified Sanskrit as the clearest, most unambiguous language for programming artificial intelligence. Panini's Ashtadhyayi (500 BCE) — a grammar of 4,000 rules — is considered the first formal computational system in human history.
Philosophy · Vedanta
🧘 Quantum Physics Echoes the Upanishads
Erwin Schrödinger, father of quantum wave theory, kept the Upanishads by his bedside. The Vedantic concept of Brahman — one infinite consciousness underlying all reality — mirrors modern quantum entanglement and unified field theory.
Architecture · Temple Science
🏛️ Temples Built Without a Single Nail
Ancient Hindu temple architects (Sthapathis) built structures that have stood for 1,000+ years without mortar or nails — using interlocking stone techniques. The Kailasa Temple at Ellora was carved from a single mountain rock — top to bottom — a feat modern engineers call virtually impossible.
